Featured Works

Modern Bahay Kubo

A three-storey modern Bahay Kubo with five bedrooms reinvents the traditional Filipino stilt house with contemporary elements and multiple levels. It maintains the core idea of a raised platform and open spaces for natural ventilation, connection to nature, and incorporates modern materials while adding vertical space and aesthetic appeal. This design features a raised ground floor, used for a parking and receiving area, with living areas on the upper floors, including bedrooms, a kitchen, and a living room to maximize living space. Large windows and a balcony offer views and natural light. It combines traditional and contemporary design, merging old and new. The interior and exterior can feature modern finishes such as glass windows, metal cladding, natural stones, and sleek architectural details for a contemporary look.

  • Location: Antipolo City
  • Year Completed: 2025
  • Client: Mr. & Mrs. Cayetano

Modern Farm House

A modern Filipino-style bungalow blends simplicity and functionality with traditional Filipino architectural elements, creating a comfortable, attractive, and engaging environment for seniors. Key features include natural materials such as wood and stone, large windows for light and airflow, and open floor plan, all with a modern aesthetic. A wide veranda or porch provides shaded outdoor space and a welcoming entrance. Modern bungalows feature an open and connected layout for the living area, dining, and kitchen, enhancing the flow and spaciousness. High ceilings contribute to a feeling of openness and help keep the interior cool. Bedrooms, bathrooms, and other functional spaces are designed for comfort, safety, and practicality, often with accessibility in mind for seniors.

  • Location: Nueva Ecija
  • Year Completed: 2023
  • Client: Mr. & Mrs. Ramos

Modern American Country House

A Modern American Country Style home features a two-storey, five-bedroom residence located in Bel-Air Laguna. It boasts simple exteriors with stone accents, porches, monotone pastel colors, pitched roofs, symmetrical designs, and architectural styles influenced by historical periods. The house features a high ceiling to maximize natural light and ventilation, along with an open layout that accommodates larger gatherings.

  • Location: Laguna
  • Year Completed: 2024
  • Client: Mr. & Mrs. Orcullo
